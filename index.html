Provider experience: plan care support with confidence

Define your needs clearly, collaborate on readiness, and reduce operational friction—without turning care into “gig work.”

  • Describe the care need (scope, timing, requirements) in a structured format.
  • Get responses aligned to skills, availability, and clinic rules—less back-and-forth.
  • Coordinate collaboration details in one place (handoff notes, checklists, confirmations).
  • Measure impact: fewer delays, smoother days, improved patient experience.

Practitioner experience: collaborate with purpose

Choose collaborations that match your skills and values—support providers, strengthen the system, and grow your career.

  • See clear expectations up front: scope, schedule, and what “good” looks like.
  • Build a collaboration profile that reflects clinical strengths and reliability.
  • Contribute to better care flow—reducing wait times and operational strain.
  • Show outcomes and contributions you can be proud of (without patient data).
